03 / Email Marketing & Customer Journeys

EMAILS THAT
EARN
EVERY CLICK.

Email remains one of the most powerful channels for building relationships, educating audiences, and driving action, when done right.

Overview

Email programs built around the customer journey, not the send calendar.

Throughout my career, I've developed email strategies that support every stage of the customer journey: from demand generation and lead nurturing to customer onboarding, product launches, webinars, events, and retention initiatives. Every campaign is built with a clear objective, thoughtful segmentation, and measurable business outcomes in mind.

The best email marketing doesn’t feel like marketing. It feels timely, relevant, and genuinely helpful.
Start with the reader

Every email begins with understanding the person receiving it: their role, their priorities, and where they are in the decision process.

Earn every click

Subject lines, preview text, CTAs: every element is crafted to earn attention and pull the reader toward the next step.

Measure what matters

Open rates are a vanity metric. I focus on clicks, conversions, pipeline influence, and downstream revenue impact.
